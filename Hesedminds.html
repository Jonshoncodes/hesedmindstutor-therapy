Our Services

    1. Home-Based Tutoring:** Our home-based tutoring services are tailored to the unique needs of each child. We understand that every child with autism has different strengths and challenges, which is why we design individualized lesson plans to cater to their learning styles. Our tutors are trained to use evidence-based teaching strategies to enhance academic skills, promote language development, and improve communication.

    2. Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Therapy: ABA therapy is a scientifically proven and effective intervention for children with autism. Our certified ABA therapists work one-on-one with each child to address behavioral challenges, develop social skills, and foster positive behaviors. We focus on building essential life skills that are necessary for daily living and social interactions.

    3. Speech-Language Therapy: Communication can be a significant barrier for children with autism. Our licensed speech-language pathologists provide targeted therapy to improve speech, language, and communication abilities. We use innovative techniques and assistive technology to enhance expressive and receptive communication skills.

    4. Occupational Therapy: Occupational therapy plays a crucial role in helping children with autism develop the necessary motor skills and sensory processing abilities. Our occupational therapists create personalized therapy plans to improve fine and gross motor skills, sensory integration, and self-regulation.

    5. Social Skills Development: Social skills are vital for building meaningful relationships and navigating social situations. Our team organizes social skills groups and activities to help children with autism develop friendships, empathy, and appropriate social behaviors.

    6. arent Training and Support: We recognize the significance of parental involvement in a child's progress. As part of our services, we offer parent training sessions to equip parents with the tools and techniques needed to support their child's learning and development at home.

    7. EP (Individualized Education Program) Consultation: For children enrolled in school, we provide IEP consultation to collaborate with educators and create comprehensive educational plans that address the child's specific needs and learning goals.

    8. Progress Monitoring and Assessment: Regular assessment and progress monitoring are integral to our approach. We use data-driven methods to track each child's development and adjust our intervention plans accordingly, ensuring continuous improvement.
